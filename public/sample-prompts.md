# Prompt Templates Batch Format
#
# Each prompt is separated by a line containing only `---`.
# The first line of each block is the **template name**.
# An optional `Tags: tag1, tag2` line can follow the name.
# Everything after that is the **prompt text**.
#
# Example:
# ─────────────────────────────────

Clean White Background

Tags: minimal, product-photo

Photograph the product centered on a pure white seamless background.
Use soft diffused lighting from above and both sides to eliminate shadows.
Ensure the product fills roughly 80% of the frame with even negative space.

---

Lifestyle Kitchen Scene

Tags: lifestyle, kitchen

Place the product on a marble countertop in a modern kitchen setting.
Natural morning light streams through a window on the left side.
Include subtle props like a coffee cup and fresh herbs to convey everyday use.

---

Dramatic Dark Mood

Tags: dramatic, dark

Product on a matte black surface with a single hard spotlight from the upper right.
Deep shadows and high contrast. Faint rim light on the left edge of the product.
Background is pure black, creating a premium luxury feel.

---

Outdoor Adventure

Tags: lifestyle, outdoor

Product held in hand against an out-of-focus mountain trail at golden hour.
Warm sunlight backlights the scene. Shallow depth of field keeps focus on the product.
Convey ruggedness and portability.
